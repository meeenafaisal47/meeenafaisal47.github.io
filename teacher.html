importance of a teacher

Teachers play a pivotal role in shaping the lives of their students, influencing not only their academic development but also their personal growth and character formation. Beyond the classroom, teachers often serve as mentors, guides, and role models, leaving an indelible impact that extends far beyond the curriculum.

role of a teacher

The major role of a teacher is to support learners in their quest for new knowledge on a specified set of subjects. This applies to students of all ages, from preschool to post-graduate learners.

what is an ideal teacher

An ideal teacher is a teacher who deeply cares about their students. An ideal teacher imparts knowledge to the students. They should also be a humble and unassuming person proving that true knowledge gives a person humility. An ideal teacher always understands their students, and helps them learn effectively.
